10 Places To Visit In Pune

When someone says Pune, a very laidback lively image evokes in my mind, and i might not be the only one who associates pune with young and vibrant crowd. indeed the cultural hub of maharashtra, Pune boasts diverse masses that eventually bring about so much of vivacity in the city that one cannot help fancy this place.

Popular tourist Attraction in Pune : - Shaniwar Wada , Aga Khan palace , Dagdushet cha Ganpati ,Sinhagad Fort ,Chaturshringi temple , Parvati hill ,Pataleshwar cave temple ,shinde Chatri. These few are the popular places in pune.
